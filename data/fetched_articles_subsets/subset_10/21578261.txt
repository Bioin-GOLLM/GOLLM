Bis(phenyl-phospho-nic) anhydride.

Li Y(1), Hua G, Slawin AM, Woollins JD.

Author information:
(1)Department of Chemistry, University of St Andrews, Fife KY16 9ST, Scotland.

The asymmetric unit of the title compound, C(12)H(12)O(5)P(2), contains four 
independent mol-ecules, generating two dimers via pairs of inter-molecular O-H⋯O 
hydrogen bonds, forming R(2) (2)(8) rings. The two aryl rings of each mol-ecule 
form dihedral angles of 108.6 (1), 103.2 (1), 12.5 (2) and 8.1 (2)° in the four 
mol-ecules.
