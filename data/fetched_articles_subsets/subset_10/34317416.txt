A Rare Presentation of Cardiomyopathy in Pregnancy.

Donald E(1), Leb J(1), Bialer M(2), Axsom K(1).

Author information:
(1)New York Presbyterian Hospital, Columbia University Irving Medical Center, 
New York, New York.
(2)Cohen Children's Medical Center, Northwell Health System, Great Neck, New 
York.

Our patient presented in her third trimester of pregnancy with new onset of 
heart failure. A thorough workup in the initial postpartum period with detailed 
past medical history, advanced imaging modalities, and a multidisciplinary 
approach revealed a rare and treatable etiology of cardiomyopathy. (Level of 
Difficulty: Intermediate.).
