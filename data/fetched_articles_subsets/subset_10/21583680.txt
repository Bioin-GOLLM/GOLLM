2,7-Dichloro-4-(chloro-acet-yl)fluorene.

Chu JB, Rao GW, Zhao JH.

There are two mol-ecules in the asymmetric unit of the title compound, 
C(15)H(9)Cl(3)O. The fluorene rings of the two mol-ecules are both coplanar 
within 066 (3) Å. In the crystal, C-H⋯O and C-H⋯Cl hydrogen bonds link the 
mol-ecules into sheets running parallel to (100).
