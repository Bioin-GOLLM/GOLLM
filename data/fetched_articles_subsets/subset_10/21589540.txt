1-(4-Chloro-benzyl-idene)-4-ethyl-thio-semicarbazide.

Li YF(1).

Author information:
(1)Microscale Science Institute, Department of Chemistry and Chemical 
Engineering, Weifang University, Weifang 261061, People's Republic of China.

In the title compound, C(10)H(12)ClN(3)S, the dihedral angle between the benzene 
ring and the thio-urea unit is 2.35 (19)°. In the crystal, inversion dimers 
linked by pairs of N-H⋯S hydrogen bonds generate R(2) (2)(8) loops.
