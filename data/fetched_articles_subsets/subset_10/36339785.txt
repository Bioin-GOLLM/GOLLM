The title mol-ecule, C19H24O5, is built by annulation of a half-chair 
cyclo-hexenone and a twist-cyclo-hexenone to a flat 4-H-pyrane boat. In the 
crystal, mol-ecules are connected via van der Waals inter-actions and C-H⋯O 
hydrogen bonds.
