(E)-3-(3,5-Dimeth-oxy-phen-yl)-1-(1-hy-droxy-naphthalen-2-yl)prop-2-en-1-one.

Lee HJ(1), Lim Y, Koh D.

Author information:
(1)Jeonju Center, Korea Basic Science Center (KBSI), Jeonju 561-765, Republic of 
Korea.

In the title mol-ecule, C21H18O4, the C=C bond of the central enone group adopts 
a trans conformation. The dihedral angle formed by the naphthalene ring system 
and the benzene ring is 2.97 (11)°. The hy-droxy group is involved in an 
intra-molecular O-H⋯O hydrogen bond. In the crystal, weak C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds 
link the mol-ecules into chains along [001].
