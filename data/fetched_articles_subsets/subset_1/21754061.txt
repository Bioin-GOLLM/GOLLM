5-Cyclo-hexyl-3-(4-fluoro-phenyl-sulfon-yl)-2-methyl-1-benzofuran.

Choi HD, Seo PJ, Son BW, Lee U.

In the title compound, C(21)H(21)FO(3)S, the cyclo-hexyl ring adopts a chair 
conformation. The 4-fluoro-phenyl ring makes a dihedral angle of 77.71 (4)° with 
the mean plane of the benzofuran fragment. In the crystal, mol-ecules are linked 
through inter-molecular C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds and aromatic π-π inter-actions 
between the furan rings of neighbouring mol-ecules [centroid-centroid distance = 
3.578 (2) Å].
