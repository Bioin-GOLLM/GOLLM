2,4-Dibromo-6-tert-butyl-benzene-1,3-diol.

Wang X, Niu JL, Zhi CX.

In the title compound, C(10)H(12)Br(2)O(2), a multiply substituted bromo-arene, 
the C-C-C angles within the aromatic ring are in the range 115.7 (7)-122.4 (7)°. 
In the crystal, mol-ecules are linked by O-H⋯O hydrogen bonds, but no π-π 
stacking is observed.
