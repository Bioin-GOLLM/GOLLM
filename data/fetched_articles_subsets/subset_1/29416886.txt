Crystal structure of the η(4)-ketimine titanium complex 
(di-phenyl-amido-κN){3-methyl-6-[(4-methyl-phen-yl)(phenyl-aza-nid-yl)methyl-idene]cyclo-hexa-2,4-dien-1-yl-κ(2)N,C(1)}(η(5)-penta-methyl-cyclo-penta-dien-yl)titanium(IV).

Fischer M(1), Schmidtmann M(1), Beckhaus R(1).

Author information:
(1)Institut für Chemie, Fakultät für Mathematik und Naturwissenschaften, Carl 
von Ossietzky Universität Oldenburg, 26129 Oldenburg, Germany.

The mol-ecular structure of the title titanium(IV) half-sandwich complex, 
[Ti(η5-C10H15)(η4-C21H19N)(C12H10N)], shows a three-legged piano-stool geometry 
at the central TiIV atom, comprising of one penta-methyl-cyclo-penta-dienyl 
ligand, one bidentate ketimine ligand in an η4-coordination mode and one 
monodentate di-phenyl-amide ligand. Except for van der Waals forces, there are 
no significant inter-molecular inter-actions in the crystal.
