Ethyl 
3-(4-meth-oxy-phen-yl)-2-phenyl-3-(4-phenyl-1,2,3-selenadiazol-5-yl)propano-ate.

Sugumar P, Sankari S, Manisankar P, Ponnuswamy MN.

In the title compound, C(26)H(24)N(2)O(3)Se, the selenadiazole ring is planar 
[maximum deviation = 0.002 (2) Å]. The dihedral angle between the selenadiazole 
ring and the attached phenyl ring is 49.00 (13)°. The crystal structure is 
stabilized by inter-molecular C-H⋯N and C-H⋯π inter-actions.
