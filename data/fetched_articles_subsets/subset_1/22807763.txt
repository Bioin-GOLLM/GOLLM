Dicarbon-yl(η(5)-cyclo-penta-dien-yl)(2,3-di-bromo-propanamine-κN)iron(II) 
tetra-fluoridoborate.

M'thiruaine CM, Friedrich HB, Changamu EO, Fernandes MA.

The title compound, [Fe(η(5)-C(5)H(5))(NH(2)CH(2)CHBrCH(2)Br)(CO)(2)](BF(4)) 
contains an Fe(II) cation with a three-legged piano-stool coordination. The 
NH(2)CH(2)CHBrCH(2)Br ligand contains a chiral carbon atom. The Fe-N bond length 
is 2.011 (3) Å and the Fe-Cp centroid distance is 1.7189 (5) Å. In the crystal, 
the ions are linked via two N-H⋯F inter-actions and a weak N-H⋯Br inter-action.
