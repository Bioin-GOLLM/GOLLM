1,4-Bis(dimethyl-silyl)-2,5-diphenyl-benzene.

Fang L, Wang R, Chen LM, Xu CH, Li SH.

The mol-ecule of the title compound, C(22)H(26)Si(2), is centrosymmetric. The 
dihedral angle between the central benzene ring and its phenyl substituents is 
67.7 (2)°. The crystal packing is stabilized by van der Waals forces.
