Susceptibility variants for male-pattern baldness on chromosome 20p11.

We carried out a genome-wide association study in 296 individuals with 
male-pattern baldness (androgenetic alopecia) and 347 controls. We then 
investigated the 30 best SNPs in an independent replication sample and found 
highly significant association for five SNPs on chromosome 20p11 (rs2180439 
combined P = 2.7 x 10(-15)). No interaction was detected with the X-chromosomal 
androgen receptor locus, suggesting that the 20p11 locus has a role in a 
yet-to-be-identified androgen-independent pathway.
