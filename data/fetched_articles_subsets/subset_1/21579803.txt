1,3,6-Trimethyl-pyrano[4,3-b]pyrrol-4(1H)-one.

Krishnakumar V, Khan FN, Hathwar VR, Nithya P, Suresh S.

All the non-H atoms of the title compound, C(10)H(11)NO(2), are almost coplanar 
[maximum deviation = 0.040 (3) Å]. The crystal structure is stabilized by C-H⋯O 
hydrogen bonds.
