Methyl 2-(4-hy-droxy-benzo-yl)benzoate.

Siddegowda MS, Jasinski JP, Golen JA, Yathirajan HS, Swamy MT.

In the title compound, C(15)H(12)O(4), the dihedral angle between the benzene 
rings is 64.0 (6)°. In the crystal, mol-ecules are linked by O-H⋯O hydrogen 
bonds, forming C(8) chains propagating in [10[Formula: see text]] and the 
packing is reinforced by weak C-H⋯O inter-actions.
