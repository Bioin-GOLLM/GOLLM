A new genus of oonopid spiders from Myanmar (Araneae, Oonopidae).

Tong Y(1), Chen H(2), Liu S(1), Li S(3).

Institute of Zoology, Chinese Academy of Sciences Beijing China.

A new genus, Kachinia Tong & Li, gen. n., including two new species, K.putao 
Tong & Li, sp. n., and K.mahmolae Tong & Li, sp. n., is described from Myanmar. 
The new genus belonging to the subfamily Oonopinae Simon, 1890, currently is the 
only member of the Brignolia-Opopaea clade with leg spines.
