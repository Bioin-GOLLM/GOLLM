Identification of two novel mutations (296 + 1G-C and A46D) in exon 2 of the 
CFTR gene in Greek cystic fibrosis patients.

Tzetis M(1), Kanavakis E, Antoniadi T, Traeger-Synodinos J, Doudounakis S, Adam 
G, Kattamis C.

Author information:
(1)First Department of Pediatrics, Athens University, Greece.

Two novel CFTR mutations were detected in Greek cystic fibrosis patients. One 
was a missense mutation, A46D, and the other a splice mutation, 296 + 1G-C. 
Neither was detected on normal chromosomes.
