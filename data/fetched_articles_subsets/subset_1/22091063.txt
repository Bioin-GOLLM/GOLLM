(8aRS)-8,8a-Dihydro-furo[3,2-f]indolizine-6,9(4H,7H)-dione.

Vrábel V, Sivý J, Svorc L, Safář P, Marchalín S.

The title compound, C(10)H(9)NO(3), is a chiral mol-ecule with one stereogenic 
carbon atom, but which crystallizes as a racemate in the centrosymmetric space 
group P2(1)/n. The central six-membered ring of the indolizine moiety adopts a 
definite envelope conformation, while the conformation of the oxopyrrolidine 
ring is close to that of a flat-envelope with a maximum deviation of 0.352 (1) Å 
for the flap atom.
