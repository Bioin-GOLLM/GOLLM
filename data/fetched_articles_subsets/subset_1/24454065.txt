The title mol-ecule, C21H20N2O2, exists in the solid state in the 'extended' 
form. The crystal packing consists of ribbons of mol-ecules extending parallel 
to c and associated via N-H⋯O and weak C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds. C-H⋯π inter-actions 
are also present.
