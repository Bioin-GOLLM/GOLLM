Crystal structure of 4-(4-meth-oxy-phen-oxy)benzaldehyde.

Schäfer A(1), Iovkova-Berends L(1), Gilke S(1), Kossmann P(1), Preut H(1), 
Hiersemann M(1).

Author information:
(1)Fakultät Chemie und Chemische Biologie, Technische Universität Dortmund, 
Otto-Hahn-Strasse 6, 44221 Dortmund, Germany.

The title compound, C14H12O3, was synthesized via the nucleophilic addition of 
4-meth-oxy-phenol to 4-fluoro-benzaldehyde. The dihedral angle between the 
least-squares planes of the benzene rings is 71.52 (3)° and the C-O-C angle at 
the central O atom is 118.82 (8)°. In the crystal, weak C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds 
link the molecules to generate supra-molecular layers in the bc plane. The 
layers are linked by weak C-H⋯π inter-actions.
