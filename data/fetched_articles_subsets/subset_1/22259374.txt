N'-(4-Hy-droxy-benzyl-idene)ferrocene-1-carbohydrazide.

Li WJ(1), Song M, Xu Y.

Author information:
(1)Department of Chemistry, Zhengzhou University, Zhengzhou 450052, People's 
Republic of China.

In the title compound, [Fe(C(5)H(5))(2)(C(13)H(11)N(2)O(2))], the dihedral angle 
between the benzene ring and the cyclo-penta-diene ring bonded to the carbonyl 
group is 26.1 (2)°. In the crystal, bifurcated O-H⋯(O,N) and N-H⋯O hydrogen 
bonds link the mol-ecules into a three-dimensional network.
