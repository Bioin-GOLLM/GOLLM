Tetra-kis(4-tert-butyl-benz-yl)silane.

Burnham LE(1), Kachlan RM, Thomas AA, Ogle CA, Jones DS.

Author information:
(1)Department of Chemistry, The University of North Carolina at Charlotte, 9201 
University City Blvd., Charlotte, NC 28223, USA.

The title compound, C(44)H(60)Si, was prepared as an inter-nal standard for 
diffusion-ordered NMR spectroscopy. The Si atom lies on a special position with 
site symmetry.
