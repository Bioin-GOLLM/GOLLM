3-Bromo-5-tert-butyl-2-hy-droxy-benz-alde-hyde.

Balasubramani V, Vinuchakkaravarthy T, Gopi S, Narasimhan S, Velmurugan D.

The mol-ecular conformation of the title compound, C(11)H(13)BrO(2), is 
stabilized by an intra-molecular O-H⋯O hydrogen bond. All non-H atoms except the 
methyl groups lie approximately in a common plane (r.m.s. deviation = 0.011 Å).
