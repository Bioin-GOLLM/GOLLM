Hexyl (E)-3-(3,4-dihy-droxy-phen-yl)acrylate.

Wang J, Gu SS, Li J, Wu FA.

The title mol-ecule, C(15)H(20)O(4), has an E conformation about its C=C bond 
and is almost planar (r.m.s. deviation of all non-H atoms = 0.04 Å). The crystal 
structurere features O-H⋯O and C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds.
