tert-Butyl 6-amino-3,4-dihydro-2H-1,4-benzoxazine-4-carboxyl-ate.

Gu XB(1), Jiang MJ, Cai GM, Zhou YY.

Author information:
(1)Key Laboratory of Nuclear Medicine, Ministry of Health, Jiangsu Key 
Laboratory of Molecular Nuclear Medicine, Jiangsu Institute of Nuclear Medicine, 
Wuxi 214063, People's Republic of China.

The title mol-ecule, C(13)H(18)N(2)O(3), contains a benzene ring fused to an 
oxazine ring and one tert-but-oxy-carbonyl group bound to the N atom of the 
oxazine ring. A weak intra-molecular C-H⋯O inter-action occurs. In the crystal, 
inter-molecular N-H⋯O and C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds stack the mol-ecules down the b 
axis. Weak C-H⋯N contacts connect the stacks, generating a three-dimensional 
network.
