(3E,5E)-3,5-Bis(naphthalen-1-yl-methyl-idene)piperidin-4-one.

Kia Y, Osman H, Murugaiyah V, Arshad S, Razak IA.

In the title compound, C(27)H(21)NO, the piperidine ring adopts a chair 
conformation. The mean plane through the piperidine ring makes dihedral angles 
of 49.27 (5) and 63.07 (5)° with the naphthalene ring systems. In the crystal, 
mol-ecules are linked into dimers via pairs of inter-molecular C-H⋯O 
inter-actions, generating ten-membered R(2) (2)(10) ring motifs. C-H⋯π 
inter-actions further stabilize the crystal structure.
