N-(4-Chloro-phen-yl)-2-(naphthalen-1-yl)acetamide.

Fun HK, Quah CK, Nayak PS, Narayana B, Sarojini BK.

In the title compound, C(18)H(14)ClNO, the naphthalene ring system [maximum 
deviation = 0.014 (9) Å] forms a dihedral angle of 74.8 (2)° with the benzene 
ring. In the crystal, mol-ecules are linked via N-H⋯O hydrogen bonds into chains 
propagating along [010].
