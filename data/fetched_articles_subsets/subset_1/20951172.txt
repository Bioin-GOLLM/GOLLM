The small ribosomal protein S12P gene rpsL as an efficient positive selection 
marker in allelic exchange mutation systems for Corynebacterium glutamicum.

Kim IK(1), Jeong WK, Lim SH, Hwang IK, Kim YH.

Author information:
(1)Laboratory of Immunobiology, School of Life Sciences and Biotechnology, 
College of Natural Sciences, Kyungpook National University, Daegu, South Korea.

We report that the mutant rpsL K43R in streptomycin-resistant and 
lysine-producing Corynebacterium glutamicum is responsible for streptomycin 
resistance. In addition, we describe its effective application in gene 
modification in C. glutamicum.
