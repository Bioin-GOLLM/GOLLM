Bis(μ-propan-2-olato-κO:O)bis-[chlor-ido(propan-2-ol-κO)bis-(pro-pan-1-ol-ato-κO)tin(IV)].

Klishin N, Brusylovets O, Brusilovets A, Rusanov E.

The binuclear centrosymmetric title compound, 
[Sn(2)(C(3)H(7)O)(6)Cl(2)(C(3)H(8)O)(2)], exhibits an edge-shared double 
octahedral exhibits an edge-shared octa-hedral structure, which is distorted 
owing to the presence of asymmetric intra-molecular hydrogen bonds between the 
axially coordinated isopropanol and isopropoxide ligands. The H atom of the 
hy-droxy group is located nearer to an isoprop-oxy group with the longest Sn-O 
bond [2.1789 (17) Å].
