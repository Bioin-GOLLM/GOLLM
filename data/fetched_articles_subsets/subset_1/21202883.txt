(1R,2R,5R,6R,9S,10S,13S,14S)-1,6,7,8,9,14,15,16,17,17-Decachloro-penta-cyclo-[12.2.1.1.0.0]octa-deca-7,15-diene.

Riddell N, McCrindle R, Arsenault G, Lough AJ.

The title compound, C(18)H(14)Cl(10), is a decachlorinated commercial flame 
retardant. The structure determination confirms the relative stereochemistry. 
The central eight-membered ring is in a chair-type conformation. In the crystal 
structure, there are no significant inter-molecular inter-actions and mol-ecules 
are separated by normal van der Waals distances.
