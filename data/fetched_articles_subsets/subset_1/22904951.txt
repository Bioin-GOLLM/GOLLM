Ethyl 1-(2-bromo-eth-yl)-3-(4-meth-oxy-phen-yl)-1H-pyrazole-5-carboxyl-ate.

Yin L, Wang Y, Wang YY, Wang JW.

In the title compound, C(15)H(17)BrN(2)O(3), the dihedral angle between the 
benzene and pyrazole rings is 5.63 (2)°. The crystal packing is stabilized by 
weak π-π stacking inter-actions [centroid-centroid distance = 3.927 (5) Å] and 
inter-molecular C-H⋯O and C-H⋯Br hydrogen bonds.
