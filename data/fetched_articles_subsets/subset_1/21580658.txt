5-Fluoro-N'-[(E)-4-methoxy-benzyl-idene]-3-phenyl-1H-indole-2-carbohydrazide.

Akkurt M, Celik I, Cihan G, Capan G, Büyükgüngör O.

In the title mol-ecule, C(23)H(18)FN(3)O(2), the mean plane of the indole system 
forms dihedral angles of 44.23 (8) and 14.54 (7)°, respectively, with the phenyl 
and benzene rings. In the crystal, inter-molecular N-H⋯O hydrogen bonds link 
mol-ecules into two-layer ribbons extended along the b axis. The crystal packing 
also exhibits weak inter-molecular C-H⋯O, C-H⋯F and C-H⋯π inter-actions.
