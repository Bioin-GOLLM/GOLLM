Reassortant avian influenza virus (H5N1) in poultry, Nigeria, 2007.

Monne I(1), Joannis TM, Fusaro A, De Benedictis P, Lombin LH, Ularamu H, Egbuji 
A, Solomon P, Obi TU, Cattoli G, Capua I.

Author information:
(1)Istituto Zooprofilattico Sperimentale delle Venezie, Legnaro, Padova, Italy 
[corrected]

Erratum in
    Emerg Infect Dis. 2008 May;14(5):865.

Genetic characterization of a selection of influenza virus (H5N1) samples, 
circulating in 8 Nigerian states over a 39-day period in early 2007, indicates 
that a new reassortant strain is present in 7 of the 8 states. Our study reports 
an entirely different influenza virus (H5N1) reassortant becoming predominant 
and widespread in poultry.
