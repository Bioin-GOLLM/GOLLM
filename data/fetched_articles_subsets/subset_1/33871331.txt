We documented 4 cases of severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 
reinfection by non-variant of concern strains among healthcare workers in 
Campinas, Brazil. We isolated infectious particles from nasopharyngeal 
secretions during both infection episodes. Improved and continued protection 
measures are necessary to mitigate the risk for reinfection among healthcare 
workers.
