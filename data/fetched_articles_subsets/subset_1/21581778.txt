(18-Crown-6-κO)(pyrazolato-κN,N')-potassium(I).

Kunz K(1), Lerner HW, Bolte M.

Author information:
(1)Institut für Anorganische Chemie, Goethe-Universität Frankfurt, 
Max-von-Laue-Str. 7, 60438 Frankfurt/Main, Germany.

The asymmetric unit of the title compound, [K(C(3)H(3)N(2))(C(12)H(24)O(6))], is 
composed of a potassium cation bonded to the six O atoms of a crown ether 
mol-ecule and the two N atoms of a pyrazolate anion. The K⋯O distances range 
from 2.8416 (8) to 3.0025 (8) Å, and the two K⋯N distances are 2.7441 (11) and 
2.7654 (11) Å. The K cation is displaced by 0.8437 (4) Å from the best plane 
through the six O atoms. The latter plane is almost perpendicular to the plane 
of the pyrazolate ring [dihedral angle 83.93 (3)°].
