2,3-Dihydro-1H-pyrrolizin-1-one.

Ali Y, Peng Y, Hua E, Anwar MA, Kalhoro MA.

There are two nearly identical mol-ecules in the asymmetric unit of the title 
compound, C(7)H(7)NO. The mol-ecules are nearly planar (r.m.s. deviations of 
0.025 and 0.017 Å) and oriented at a dihedral angle of 28.98 (3)°. The two 
mol-ecules are linked by a C-H⋯O hydrogen bond. In the crystal, weak 
inter-molecular C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds link the mol-ecules into zigzag chains 
along the c axis.
