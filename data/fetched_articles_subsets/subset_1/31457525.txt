Extending the Scope of a New Cyanation: Design and Synthesis of an Anthracene 
Derivative with an Exceptionally Low LUMO Level and Improved Solubility.

Glöcklhofer F(1), Morawietz AJ(1), Stöger B(2), Unterlass MM(3), Fröhlich J(1).

Author information:
(1)Institute of Applied Synthetic Chemistry, TU Wien, Getreidemarkt 9/163, 1060 
Vienna, Austria.
(2)Institute of Chemical Technologies and Analytics, TU Wien, Getreidemarkt 
9/164, 1060 Vienna, Austria.
(3)Institute of Materials Chemistry, TU Wien, Getreidemarkt 9/165, 1060 Vienna, 
Austria.

The preparation of cyanated acenes from quinones has been improved for the 
conversion of electron-poor starting materials. The new procedure was used to 
prepare rationally designed 2,7-dinitro-9,10-dicyanoanthracene. 
Crystallographic, morphological, and electrochemical investigations have 
revealed most promising properties for applications in organic electronics.
