Acridinium 3,5-dicarboxy-benzoate monohydrate.

Derikvand Z, Aghabozorg H, Attar Gharamaleki J.

The title compound, C(13)H(10)N(+)·C(9)H(5)O(6) (-)·H(2)O, exhibits a wide range 
of non-covalent inter-actions, such as O-H⋯O and N-H⋯O hydrogen bonds, π-π 
stacking [centroid-centroid distances = 3.562 (8) and 3.872 (8) Å] and ion 
pairing, connecting the various components into a supra-molecular structure.
