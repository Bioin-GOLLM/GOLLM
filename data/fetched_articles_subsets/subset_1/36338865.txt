The title compound, 5-hy-droxy-N,N,N-tri-methyl-tryptammonium (5-HTQ) iodide 
{systematic name: [2-(5-hy-droxy-1H-indol-3-yl)eth-yl]tri-methyl-aza-nium 
iodide}, C13H19N2O+·I-, has a single tryptammonium cation and one iodide anion 
in the asymmetric unit. The ions are held together by N-H⋯I and O-H⋯I hydrogen 
bonds in infinite chains along [100].
