In the title GaIII complex compound with pentetic acid, 
[Ga(C14H20N3O10)(H2O)]·3H2O, the GaIII centre is bound in a slightly distorted 
octa-hedral coordination sphere by two amine N atoms, three carboxyl-ate O atoms 
and one water O atom. The complex mol-ecule exists as a zwitterion. In the 
crystal, the complexes are linked to each other via O-H⋯O and C-H⋯O hydrogen 
bonds, forming layers parallel to (001). Three uncoordinating water mol-ecules 
link the complex layers via O-H⋯O, N-H⋯O and C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds, forming a 
three-dimensional network.
