Diiodido{2-[(4-meth-oxy-phen-yl)imino-meth-yl]pyridine-κ(2)N,N'}zinc.

Salehzadeh S, Khalaj M, Dehghanpour S.

In the title complex, [ZnI(2)(C(13)H(12)N(2)O)], the Zn(II) atom has a distorted 
tetra-hedral coordination. The organic ligand is bidentate, coordinating the 
Zn(II) atom via the two N atoms. The benzene and pyridine rings are oriented at 
a dihedral angle of 11.67 (9)°. In the crystal, weak C-H⋯I and C-H⋯O hydrogen 
bonds are observed, in addition to π-π stacking inter-actions, with a 
centroid-centroid distance of 3.72 (5) Å.
