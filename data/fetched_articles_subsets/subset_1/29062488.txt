We report the isolation of three bacterial strains that could not be identified 
by matrix-assisted laser desorption/ionization time-of-flight mass spectrometry 
screening. 'Gracilibacillus phocaeensis' sp. nov., 'Sediminibacillus 
massiliensis' sp. nov. and 'Virgibacillus ndiopensis' sp. nov. are halophilic 
species isolated from salty human stools by culturomics.
