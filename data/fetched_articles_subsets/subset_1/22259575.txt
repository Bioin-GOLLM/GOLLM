(1E,4E)-1-(3-Nitro-phen-yl)-5-phenyl-penta-1,4-dien-3-one.

Samshuddin S, Butcher RJ, Akkurt M, Narayana B, Sarojini BK, Yathirajan HS.

In the title compound, C(17)H(13)NO(3), the dihedral angle between the benzene 
rings is 31.21 (5)°. In the crystal, inversion dimers linked by pairs of C-H⋯O 
hydrogen bonds occur. A C-H⋯π inter-action is also indicated.
