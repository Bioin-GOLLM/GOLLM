2-tert-Butyl-4-chloro-5-[4-(2-fluoro-eth-oxy)benz-yloxy]pyridazin-3(2H)-one.

Jing H(1), Mou T, Zhang X.

Author information:
(1)Key Laboratory of Radiopharmaceuticals, Ministry of Education, College of 
Chemistry, Beijing Normal University, 19 Xinjiekou Outer St, Beijing 100875, 
People's Republic of China.

In the title compound, C(17)H(20)ClFN(2)O(3), the dihedral angle between the 
pyridazine and benzene rings is 41.37 (10)°. In the crystal, there are no 
significant intermolecular interactions present. The terminal -CH(2)F group is 
disordered over two sets of sites with an occupancy ratio of 
0.737 (2):0.263 (2).
