N-(4-Bromo-phen-yl)-2-(naphthalen-1-yl)acetamide.

Fun HK, Quah CK, Narayana B, Nayak PS, Sarojini BK.

In the title compound, C(18)H(14)BrNO, the naphthalene ring system and the 
benzene ring form dihedral angles of 78.8 (2) and 19.7 (2)°, respectively, with 
the acetamide C-C(=O)-N plane. The naphthalene ring system forms a dihedral 
angle of 64.88 (19)° with the benzene ring. In the crystal, mol-ecules are 
linked via inter-molecular bifurcated (N,C)-H⋯O hydrogen bonds, generating an 
R(2) (1)(6) ring motif, forming chains along the b axis.
