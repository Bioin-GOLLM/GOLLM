An ortho-rhom-bic polymorph of 2-(1,3-benzothia-zol-2-yl)-6-eth-oxy-phenol.

Kargar H, Kia R, Sharafi Z, Jahromi HJ, Tahir MN.

In the title mol-ecule, C(15)H(13)NO(2)S, an intra-molecular O-H⋯N hydrogen bond 
forms an S(6) ring motif. The benzothia-zole ring system and the benzene ring 
form a dihedral angle of 8.9 (3) Å. In the crystal, mol-ecules are linked by 
weak C-H⋯O hydrogen bonds, forming chains along the b axis. In addition, π-π 
inter-actions [centroid-centroid distances = 3.772 (4) and 3.879 (4) Å] are 
observed.
