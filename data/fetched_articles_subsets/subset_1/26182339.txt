First Detection of a Splice Acceptor Site β-Thalassemia Mutation: IVS-I-130 
(HBB: c.93-1G > C) in a Chinese Patient.

He S(1), Zhang Q, Zheng C, Wei Y, Tang Y, Chen Q, Chen S.

Author information:
(1)Prenatal Diagnosis Center, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Women and 
Children Care Hospital , Nanning , People's Republic of China.

We present the first description of a Chinese patient with a rare β-thalassemia 
(β-thal) mutation IVS-I-130 (HBB: c.93-1G > C). This mutation is a splice donor 
site mutation, and is associated with a β(0)-thal phenotype.
