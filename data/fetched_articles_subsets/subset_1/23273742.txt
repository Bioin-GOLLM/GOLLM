Hsp70 ATP binding induces substrate release, but the transiency of this state 
has inhibited its characterization. In this issue, Kityk et al. determine the 
Hsp70(∗)ATP structure utilizing engineered disulfide bonds, providing insights 
into the workings of this essential molecular machine.
