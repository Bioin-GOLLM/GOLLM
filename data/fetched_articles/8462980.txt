A novel nonsense mutation in the protein C (PROC) gene (Trp-29-->term) causing 
recurrent venous thrombosis.

Millar DS(1), Grundy CB, Bignell P, Mitchell DC, Corden D, Woods P, Kakkar VV, 
Cooper DN.

Author information:
(1)Charter Molecular Genetics Laboratory, Thrombosis Research Institute, 
Chelsea, London, UK.

A novel heterozygous TGG-->TAG (Trp-29-->Term) substitution was detected in 
three members of a family with inherited type 1 protein C deficiency and 
recurrent venous thrombosis.
