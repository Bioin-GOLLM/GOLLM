We report here the main characteristics of a new bacterium named 'Beduinella 
massiliensis' strain Marseille-P2846(T) (CSURP2846P) that was isolated from a 
faecal specimen of a 50-year-old Saudi Bedouin female and propose the creation 
of a new family 'Beduinellaceae'.
