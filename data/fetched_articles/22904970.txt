1-(Adamantan-1-yl)-3-(4-meth-oxy-phenyl)prop-2-en-1-one.

Nguyen TM(1), Fronczek FR, Watkins SF.

Author information:
(1)Department of Chemistry, Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, LA 
70803-1804, USA.

The title mol-ecule, C(20)H(24)O(2), is a chalconoid derivative in which the 
keto-enone group is slightly distorted from planarity; the O=C-C=C torsion angle 
is 12.24 (13)°.
